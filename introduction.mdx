This documentation covers how to participate in competitions, use the WCA website, integrate with the WCA API, and contribute to the platform.

## What is the WCA platform?

The World Cube Association website is a full-stack Ruby on Rails application with a Next.js frontend that powers all official WCA operations:

- **Competition management** — Organizers and delegates manage the full lifecycle of competitions, from submission through announcement to results posting
- **Competitor registration** — Athletes register for competitions with integrated Stripe and PayPal payment support
- **Official results and rankings** — All competition results feed into global, continental, and national rankings updated after every event
- **Public API** — A REST API (v0 and v1) lets developers build tools, apps, and integrations on top of WCA data
- **Live results** — Real-time result entry and display during active competitions
- **Data exports** — Full public database exports in SQL and TSV format, updated regularly

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a WCA account">
    Sign up at [worldcubeassociation.org](https://www.worldcubeassociation.org/users/sign_up) to access competitions, track your results, and claim your WCA ID.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find a competition">
    Browse upcoming competitions on the [competitions page](https://www.worldcubeassociation.org/competitions). Filter by location, date, and events.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register and compete">
    Register for a competition, receive confirmation, and compete. Your results are posted to the official database after the event.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the API">
    Use the [WCA API](/api/overview) to access competition data, results, rankings, and more in your own applications.
  </Step>
</Steps>
